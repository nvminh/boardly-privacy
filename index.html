Privacy Policy

Boardly — Board Game Collection

Last updated: May 9, 2025

Plain language summary

Boardly does not collect, store, or sell your personal information. All your data stays on your device. The only third-party data collection comes from ads served by Google AdMob.

1. Who We Are

Boardly (com.nvm.boardly) is a free mobile board-game app. When this policy says "we", "us", or "our", it refers to the developer of Boardly.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store on any server:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information (there are no accounts)
  • Your location
  • Your game history or move data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device content

Everything you enter in the app — your player nickname, avatar, board theme preference, and game history — is saved locally on your device only using Android's standard app storage. It never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Local Network (LAN Multiplayer)

When you start or join a local-network game, the app broadcasts your chosen nickname and avatar emoji over your Wi-Fi network so nearby players can find you. This data is sent only within your local network and is never routed to the internet or stored on any server.

The app uses your device's camera solely to scan QR codes for joining a game. No images are captured, stored, or transmitted.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Boardly is free and is supported by ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use your device's Advertising ID and other information to show relevant ads. This data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

5. Permissions Explained

  • INTERNET / NETWORK STATE / WIFI STATE — Required to load ads and to discover other players on the local network.
  • CHANGE_WIFI_MULTICAST_STATE — Required for LAN game discovery via mDNS/Bonjour.
  • CAMERA — Used only to scan QR codes for joining a local game. No images are stored.

6. Children's Privacy

Boardly contains only abstract board games with no violent, sexual, or otherwise age-restricted content. However, because the app serves ads through Google AdMob, it is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue or send a message via the Play Store listing.
